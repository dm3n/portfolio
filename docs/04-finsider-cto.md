# Finsider — Chief Technology Officer

> [finsider.ai](https://finsider.ai) · full‑time CTO

Daniel joined Finsider — a funded financial‑AI company with an existing product and customers — as **full‑time CTO**, bringing the M&A‑intelligence thesis and agentic‑systems expertise developed across Flagpost and Airbank into an operating fintech.

## Mandate
As CTO, the role spans the full technical surface: product engineering across a multi‑repo platform (frontend, backend engine, and AI/agent services), the financial‑correctness layer that the product's credibility depends on, the developer‑productivity and delivery infrastructure, and the AI/agent strategy.

## Headline contribution — Symphony
The signature build is **[Symphony](02-symphony-architecture.md)**, a Jira‑native autonomous software‑development runner deployed live in Finsider's active development environment. It takes product tickets through implementation, validation, visual‑evidence capture, human review, and draft‑PR submission — with safe git operations, cross‑repo scope critics, design‑system guardrails, and bounded failure recovery. Symphony is, in effect, the company's force multiplier for shipping: *tooling that builds the product*, engineered to the standard of the production codebase it operates on.

## Platform & correctness work
Alongside Symphony, the CTO work includes core product engineering on Finsider's financial platform:

- **Financial statement correctness** — the hierarchy and tie‑out of Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow, and Cash Proof in the product UI, plus export to standardized financial‑formatting rules. This is the part of a financial‑AI product where trust is won or lost; it requires the same auditability discipline as the Airbank QoE engine.
- **Product surface & UX** — entity pages, reporting toolbars, and a consistent design system (shared primitives, a global font standard) across the application.
- **Support & operations automation** — an in‑app feedback/support flow that turns user reports into tracked tickets with team notifications, closing the loop between users and engineering.
- **Delivery infrastructure** — branch hygiene, CI‑aware workflows, and the agentic delivery pipeline (Symphony) that standardizes how work goes from ticket to reviewed PR.

## How it connects to the rest of this portfolio
Finsider is where the three threads converge: the **research** (why long autonomous chains fail), the **agentic engineering** (Symphony's supervised‑autonomy design), and the **financial‑AI domain depth** (auditable, source‑linked correctness) — applied at the scale of a funded company with real customers.
